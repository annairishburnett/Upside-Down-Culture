Kalamazoo Flyers

I love flyer art and I have many exceptional examples of awesome flyers by so many great and often unknown artists from my time spent in Kalamazoo! If you would like me to share which ones are yours I will certainly try! Though I am not making any promises it will happen in a timely manner, since there’s just so many to try and archive! Thanks for your understanding.

The Kalamazoo flyers here will initially draw from my personal archive, and are loosely in chronological order…PLEASE, if you have any from any era that you do not see here email upsidedownculture@gmail.com, along with any details you may have regarding who booked the event, who made the flyer, etc. Thanks!
